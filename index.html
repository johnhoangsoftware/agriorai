About US

What is Agriblock ?

Agriblock is a Web3 blockchain project with a focus on agricultural traceability aimed at promoting transparency in food safety. It integrates and provides artificial intelligence (AI) services to detect pests and forecast weather to support farmers.

The problem that

We Are Solving

Web3

Solve the problem of transparency in farming and animal husbandry by storing on the blockchain

AI - Artificial intelligence

Using AI to detect plant pests, weather forecasts and fake reviews

Blockchain

Information storage, transactions are safe, secure, public and transparent

Artificial Intelligence - AI

Providing artificial intelligence services to farmers includes assistance in detecting pests early, guidance on how to effectively deal with pests, and advice on plant care according to industry standards. Additionally, the service offers tools such as weather forecasting, production forecasting, and production process automation.

Check-in & shop to earn

Users can receive project tokens by checking in (taking pictures) at farms. Additionally, they can earn tokens by shopping for agricultural products on the site, and writing product reviews can also earn them tokens.
